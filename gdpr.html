Privacy & GDPR Policy

Information We Collect

We are committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), we only collect data that is strictly necessary for providing our telecommunications and telemetry services. This includes contact details, network identifiers, and standard system logs.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is exclusively used to maintain secure connections, ensure route managing service (QRMS) functionality, and provide essential customer support. We do not sell your personal data to third parties. All internal processing is done in compliance with UK and EU data protection laws.

Data Security

Security is at the core of our QRMS link protocol. We encrypt all telemetry and personal data using link-unique random keys. Your data is stored on secure servers with restricted access, meaning that even in the unlikely event of a breach, intercepted data remains cryptographically secure.

Your Rights

  • The right to be informed about how your data is being used.
  • The right of access to your personal data.
  • The right to rectification of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • The right to erasure (the "right to be forgotten").
  • The right to restrict processing.
  • The right to data portability.
  • The right to object to automated decision-making and profiling.

If you have any questions regarding your data or wish to exercise your rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@cellxion.net
